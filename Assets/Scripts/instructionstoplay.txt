You will be given a pattern that you must tap and repeat.
Repeat the pattern by tapping the spacebar.
If you successfully repeat the pattern, you receive points!
If you are playing in visual only mode: watch the pattern that comes from the speakers.
Watch the white beats, and when you see the green beat, that's your signal to repeat the beat!
If you are playing in audio only mode, listen to the beat given
When you hear "go!", repeat the beat!
If you are playing without these modes, use both cues to repeat the patterns!
Good luck, and get groovin'!
